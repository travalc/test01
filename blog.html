Jiu-Jitsu

Jiu Jitsu is a grappling martial art that is originally created in Japan and later evolved in Brazil. The most popular form of Jiu Jitsu practiced today is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. It emphasizes the use of body position and leverage, along with good technique, over brute strength when overcoming an opponent. It is a great form of self defense and is highly practical.
